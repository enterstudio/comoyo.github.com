Who are we?

Comoyo delivers internet services that make users’ lives easier and more fun. Currently our main focus areas are TV/Film streaming services and internet-based communication services. We build services for global scale, while using local market knowledge to adapt content to local needs and tastes.

As an independent subsidiary of Telenor, Comoyo is responsible for all product development of internet services for the consumer market in the Telenor Group. Our products will be sold both in the open market and through Telenor’s operators worldwide.

What drives us?

We want to make life on the internet easier, richer, and more fun. Our goal is to create useful services that people rely on every day. Since change is the only constant, we believe that a product is never finished – it is only a representation of users’ needs at the time. We build backend and infrastructure components that scale and can be reused across products and countries, allowing us to roll out new services quickly. Achieving scale is important to allow us to offer our users affordable services and the best content.

Some of the values that guide our work

We want to build a culture based on strong values. Here are some of them.

  1. We continuously improve our skills - by doing things, not talking or making powerpoints
  2. We constantly challenge ourselves and seek out situations and people who can challenge us
  3. We build and operate our core components. We combine open source and self-written software as appropriate, but keep competence and ability to change, improve, and adapt.
  4. We don't launch and forget. An unmaintained component is a dying, rotting component.
  5. No code is known by only one person. We use code reviews and pair programming to spread knowledge.
  6. We give each other direct and honest feedback about product/code quality etc. Feedback is used as an opportunity for learning and improving.
  7. According to agile principles, we don't start out with a fat spec, but make working programs in small steps. We get early feedback and adjust the direction we're going in.
  8. After each iteration, we analyze what we can learn and how we should change.

Hiring process

Our hiring process is designed to find people who fit our culture and can bring world-class skills to our Engineering team.

Who are you?

The ideal candidate has product development experience building dependable, large scale distributed software systems. In addition to excellent problem solving skills, candidates should have strong algorithm skills and be proficient in all areas of software development – analysis, design, implementation, test, performance tuning, debugging, and operations.

We hire internationally, so all candidates may apply.

The process

  1. You submit your resume.
  2. Review by engineers of candidate CVs.
  3. Relevant candidates will be asked to do a programming task for a real-world scenario (approx 4 hours), or give examples in the form of relevant contributions to e.g. open source projects.
  4. Engineering will review submitted code (3) and invite the best candidates to an interview.
  5. Interviews (In person, over video / Skype).
  6. Reference check.
  7. An offer or rejection is given.
